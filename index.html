Professor Dele Olanisimi

Professor Bamidele Oladapo Olanisimi is a distinguished academic and a key figure at Tai Solarin University of Education (TASUED) in Nigeria. He is the Director of the Centre for Human Rights and Gender Education (CHURGE) at TASUED, where he plays a significant role in promoting human rights and gender education through various initiatives. One of his notable contributions includes partnering with the Ogun State Judiciary to train practitioners in Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR)【6†source】【7†source】. Prof. Olanisimi's work is crucial in fostering peaceful resolutions and legal education within the community.
